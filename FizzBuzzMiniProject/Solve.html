FizzBuzz

Enter 2 values and we'll check them against numbers 1-100. For each multiple of the first number, you'll get a Fizz! For each multiple of the second number... You guessed it! You get a Buzz! For every number that is a multiple of the first and second number, you get a FizzBuzz!
